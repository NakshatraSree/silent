About Us / Team

Our Shuttle Bus Tracking system uses GPS technology to provide real-time tracking of shuttle buses within our college's campus. Our application aims to minimize waiting times and display the exact timings when the shuttle buses would be operating between AB1 and AB3

About the Project / Team

The Shuttle Bus Tracking system is an innovative application that brings great efficiency to shuttle bus operations. With our real-time tracking technology, students can know the exact location of their shuttle buses, making their daily travelling experience more accurate.
